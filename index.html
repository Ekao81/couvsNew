ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Connor LaCouvee. With years of experience in the AHL, ECHL, and Europe, I'm deeply committed to guiding the upcoming generation of goalies. At the heart of my coaching is a holistic approach, rooted in three core principles:

Technical: Beyond just fundamental skills, I emphasize the nuances of every move, ensuring that each goalie's technique is both fluid and precise.

Tactical: A goalie's prowess isn't just in the save, but in anticipating the play. By enhancing goaltending IQ, I help goalies develop a proactive mindset, allowing them to read the game several moves ahead.

Mental: In the high-stakes world of goaltending, mental strength is as crucial as physical agility. We'll work on fostering unwavering confidence, cultivating strategies to remain centered in high-pressure moments, and ensuring a mental resilience that's second to none.

Together, this comprehensive approach ensures that goalies under my guidance are not just robotic athletes but strategic thinkers, well-rounded and prepared for any challenge on the ice. Let's harness the full spectrum of your potential and redefine excellence between the pipes.
