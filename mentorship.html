Mentorship program

Welcome to the LaCouvee Goaltending Mentorship Program, expertly crafted for goalies eager to refine their skills, receive career guidance, and achieve personal growth. Spearheaded by Connor LaCouvee, a seasoned professional with six years of top-level experience, this program offers an unparalleled opportunity for goalies to enhance their game under expert tutelage.

DETAILS

What We Offer:

Expert Career Guidance: Draw from Connor’s rich experience to navigate your goaltending career, making informed decisions at every turn.

Skill Development: Participate in one-on-one Zoom sessions for in-depth analysis of game film, honing your techniques and understanding of goalie tactics.

Mental Strategies: Learn how to build mental toughness, maintain focus in high-pressure situations, and develop resilience, crucial skills for any goalie.

Nutrition and Fitness: Get access to tailored off-ice training and nutrition programs, designed to optimize your physical capabilities and ensure you’re always at your best.

Monthly Guest Speakers: Be inspired and gain insights from a series of guest speakers from the goaltending and broader sporting community.

Your Mentor - Connor LaCouvee: Connor LaCouvee brings a wealth of experience from his six years in professional goaltending. His mentorship goes beyond the technical aspects of the game, offering personalized advice and support that covers all facets of life as a goalie.

Who Should Join:

This program is ideally suited for goalies at any level who are serious about advancing their careers, sharpening their skills, and growing both personally and professionally in the dynamic world of goaltending.
